üîç Important Exam Points
  • DDREF = 2 - Always applied to solid cancer risks
  • 5% per Sv - Standard fatal cancer risk for protection
  • Heritable risk reduced 6-8 fold from ICRP-60
  • Whole population values higher than adult-only values due to children's sensitivity
  • Detriment concept includes non-fatal cancers and quality of life
  • Risk coefficients apply to low-LET radiation (X-rays, gamma rays)
üìã Risk Coefficient Components

Detriment includes:

  • Probability of fatal cancer
  • Weighted probability of non-fatal cancer
  • Relative length of life lost
  • Probability of severe heritable effects
Weighting for non-fatal cancers: Accounts for reduced quality of life, with weights varying by cancer type (e.g., thyroid cancer weight = 0.1, breast cancer weight = 0.5).
